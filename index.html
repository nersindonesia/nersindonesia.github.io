History

NERS Indonesia was established on Monday 11 November 2013, which was initiated by several people who are active in disaster management, and was founded with the hope that in the future this voluntary organization can provide benefits.


Initially, NERS Indonesia had the name Ners_peduli, but in 2017 it changed its full name to NERS Indonesia. Members who are members of NERS Indonesia are called volunteers, and every volunteer who joins is not paid by the organization, or anyone, all on the basis of their willingness to help others according to their requirements. with the scientific discipline.


NERS Indonesia since its establishment until now continues to be active in providing contributions and assistance with ideas, energy and thoughts both in disaster areas and in areas needed as an effort in collaborative management with other agencies or institutions.

Funding

NERS Indonesia have funding from donors, CSR, and business aspects made by volunteers including National or International seminars conducted as foundraising so that later the results from these activities will be given 100% for the organization's operations and assistance needs in disaster areas.


NERS Indonesia Under the auspices of the Foundation of Humanity Volunteers, the management of the Center is located in Cisarua, Bogor Regency, in accordance with the notarial deed.
